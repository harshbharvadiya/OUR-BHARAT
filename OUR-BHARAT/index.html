India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the most populous country in the world, with over 1.4 billion people. India is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and unique geography.


KEY INFORMATION ABOUT INDIA:

1. GEOGRAPHY:

Capital: New Delhi

Borders: Pakistan to the northwest, China and Nepal to the north, Bhutan to the northeast, Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. It is bounded by the Indian Ocean to the south.

Major Rivers: Ganges, Yamuna, Brahmaputra, Godavari, Krishna, and Narmada

Climate: Ranges from tropical in the south to temperate in the north. India experiences four primary seasons: winter, summer, monsoon, and post-monsoon.
